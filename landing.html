America Through The Journalist Glass

The Smithsonian Revolutionary America from 1770-1809 VOL. I

About The
Project

This project follows a database of journalistic issues from a variety of news publications from multiple states in Revolutionary America. The purpose of the project is to view this time in our country through a journalistic perspective. What states were pushing out the most information? What was the information? What years were the most information condensed? All these questions are meant to push the user into an immersive experience where they can view the information that was being shared at the time.
